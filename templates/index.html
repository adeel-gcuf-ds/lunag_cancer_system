About LungScan AI

LungScan AI is an advanced lung cancer detection system that uses deep learning to analyze CT scan images and identify different types of lung cancer with high accuracy.

Our system can detect three types of lung cancer:

  • Lung Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC) - A type of non-small cell lung cancer
  • Lung Adenocarcinoma (ACA) - The most common type of lung cancer
  • Normal Lung Tissue (N) - For comparison with healthy tissue
